### 3. Tested four levers to "trade more + profit"
| Lever | Result |
|---|---|
| Sentiment (`p55_s20`) | Weak / marginally dilutive (PF 1.82 → 1.76) |
| Regime loosening (z 0.5→0) | **Worse** — fewer trades, lower Sharpe |
| Cooldown {0,1,3,5} | Not the bottleneck — `occupied` ≫ `cooldown` (~6:1) → **one-position-per-symbol** is the real constraint |
| Universe expansion (+GD/RTX/LHX/TLT/IEF) | +62% trades, PF 1.93 at live z≥1.0 gate |

### 4. Beta-isolation check on universe expansion → it's BETA, not alpha
- Per-trade test (`backtest/defence_beta_isolation.py`): mean alpha ≈ 0 (p=0.62/0.23), only ~35% of trades beat a random same-length hold (sign-test p<0.005). Defence-prime basket B&H = +99.8%, Sharpe 0.60 ≈ strategy 0.66.
- **Conclusion:** the gains were the 2022–2025 rearmament drift, not timing skill. No lever produced genuine alpha.

### 5. Took the beta deliberately — defence sleeve
- Prototype `backtest/defence_sleeve_overlay.py`: a **separate buy-and-hold defence-ETF sleeve** blended with the gated strategy. **PPA** chosen (best return/DD/Sharpe). Synk↔PPA correlation ≈ **+0.012** → blended Sharpe **0.43 → 0.82** at 15% (DD −7.7%).
- **Decision: PPA sleeve at 15–20% weight.**

### 6. Built the sleeve execution module (paper-first, ships DISABLED)
- `execution/sleeve_executor.py`: pure `compute_rebalance()` + `rebalance_sleeve(cfg)`; quarterly + drift band; independent of kill switch; own state/log; market-open guard.
- `config.py` + `.env.example`: `SLEEVE_*` settings (default off, bound to `ALPACA_PAPER`).
- **Carve-outs** (sleeve held outside gated logic): `main.job_strategy` + `order_executor.check_exits` exclude `SLEEVE_SYMBOL`; `risk/kill_switch.py` uses **gated equity = total − sleeve value** (thresholds unchanged 2/5/30).
- `main.py`: daily `sleeve_rebalance` job (self-gates). `tests/sleeve_test.py`: pure unit tests.
